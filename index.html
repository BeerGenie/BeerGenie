About BeerGenie

BeerGenie is the intelligent blood alcohol content (BAC) tracker designed to help you make informed decisions about drinking. Whether you're at a bar, party, or home, BeerGenie provides real-time BAC estimates to keep you safe.

Our app uses scientifically-backed formulas and personalized calculations based on your weight, height, biological sex, and individual drinking patterns. Track your sessions, monitor your patterns, and stay safe while partying responsibly.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Safety Notice:

BAC calculations are estimates based on general formulas and may not reflect your actual blood alcohol content. Many factors affect BAC including metabolism, food intake, medications, and individual physiology. This app should NOT be used to determine fitness to drive or operate machinery. Always err on the side of caution. Never drink and drive.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

BeerGenie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Date of birth (to verify legal drinking age)
  • Biological sex (used for BAC calculation accuracy)
  • Height and weight (used for BAC calculation accuracy)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Drinking session data: Drinks logged, timestamps, and calculated BAC levels
  • App usage data: Features used and session duration
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version

Advertising Data

If you use the ad-supported version of our app, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate your Blood Alcohol Content (BAC) accurately
  • Provide personalized hydration reminders and safety notifications
  • Track your drinking history and provide statistics
  • Display relevant advertisements (free version)
  • Improve our app and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud & Local Storage: Your profile and session history sync to the cloud (Firebase/Firestore) and may be cached locally for offline access.
  • Secure Storage: Passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication and secured per Google's standards; sensitive tokens are stored in iOS Keychain where applicable.
  • Your Control: You can delete your account and associated data from within the app; uninstalling the app removes local caches.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Advertising)

Used to display advertisements in the free version. Has its own privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

Used to process the "Remove Ads" purchase. Handled entirely by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Firebase (Authentication & Database)

Firebase Authentication manages sign-in and credentials. Firestore stores user profile and session history to sync across devices. See Google's privacy policy for Firebase services: Google Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data (cloud and local caches)
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized advertising
  • Data Portability: Data is stored in Firebase and cached locally; you may request export via the contact email below

Children's Privacy

BeerGenie is intended for users of legal drinking age only. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. If you are under the legal drinking age, please do not use this app.

Age Verification

By using BeerGenie, you confirm that you are of legal drinking age in your country or region of residence.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
